About us

Coedlan Land and Woodland Services is passionate about sustainable land and woodland management. Our small team, led by Luke, draw from a wide range of experiences to provide the best solution to your problem. We aim to complete work with a minimum of disruption to you, your neighbours and the nearby wildlife.

Our equipment is regularly serviced and maintained in accordance with the FISA guidelines. Our team are trained and competant. Where appropriate, our team hold the relevant NPTC qualifications.
